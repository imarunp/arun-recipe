Masu ko achar recipe

Masu ko achar or Meat Pickle is possibly the best pickle out there which you can make at home easily. If you are a meat lover like me, you are going to get crazy trying out this recipe.

Note: You might get poor buying meat again and again after this.

Ingredients

  • A glass jar to store
  • Boneless chicken meat
  • All masalas you want to add
  • Oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cut meat into small pieces
  2. Fry the meat till its red
  3. Fry the masalas if needed
  4. Mix meat and masala
  5. Let it cool and add lemon. ENJOY heaven
